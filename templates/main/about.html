About AgroHands AI

Fertilizer inventory management for rural shops

Rural fertilizer shops often face stock shortages and errors due to manual bookkeeping. AgroHands AI uses AI-based text recognition (OCR) to extract item details from uploaded bills, update stock automatically, and support digital payments so farmers get fertilizers on time.

We help reduce human error, prevent stock-outs, and bring transparency to inventory and billing.
